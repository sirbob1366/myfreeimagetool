Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

MyFreeImageTool ("we", "us") is built around a simple idea: your images are yours. This policy explains what is and isn't collected when you use myfreeimagetool.com.

1. Your images

Every tool on this site runs entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images are processed on your own device and are never uploaded to, stored on, or transmitted to our servers. When you close the tab, nothing about your image remains with us.

2. Information we collect

  • Usage analytics. Aggregated, non-identifying data such as which tools are used and general browser type, to improve the site. This never includes your image contents.
  • Advertising data. We may show ads to keep the site free; ad partners may use cookies (see below).
  • Messages you send us. If you email us, we receive what you include.

We do not offer user accounts and do not sell your personal information.

3. Cookies & advertising

We use local storage for essentials (like remembering your light/dark theme) and may use cookies for analytics and advertising. You can clear cookies in your browser at any time without breaking the tools.

4. Third-party services

The site loads open-source libraries (such as pdf-lib for Image-to-PDF) and may use analytics/advertising providers that operate under their own privacy policies. The image-processing itself runs locally and transmits nothing.

5. Children's privacy

The site is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

6. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes are reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact

Questions? Email hello@myfreeimagetool.com or visit our Contact page.
