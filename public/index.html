Frequently asked questions

Is MyFreeImageTool really free?

Yes. Every tool — including AI background removal, OCR and 4K collages — is free with no signup, no watermark and no limits. The site is supported by unobtrusive ads.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Every tool, including the AI ones, runs entirely in your browser using the Canvas API and WebAssembly — your images never leave your device. Most tools keep working offline once the page has loaded.

How can AI tools work without a server?

The models (background matting, face detection, OCR) are compact enough to download to your browser — a few megabytes each, fetched once and cached. Your hardware runs them, which is why there are no per-use limits.

What formats are supported?

JPG, PNG and WebP everywhere; plus HEIC (iPhone), AVIF, SVG, GIF, BMP, TIFF and ICO in the converters, and PDF output from images.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The site is fully responsive and works on iPhone, iPad and Android. Heavy jobs run at the speed of your device.
