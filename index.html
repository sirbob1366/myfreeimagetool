Frequently asked questions

Is MyFreeImageTool really free?

Yes. Every tool is free with no signup, no watermark and no limits. The site is supported by unobtrusive ads.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Every tool runs entirely in your browser using the Canvas API — your images never leave your device.

What formats are supported?

JPG, PNG and WebP for editing and conversion. The Image-to-PDF tool turns those images into a PDF.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The site is fully responsive and works on iPhone, iPad and Android.

Do I need to install anything?

No. It runs in any modern browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge. Nothing to install.
